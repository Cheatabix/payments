## What this calculator does

Instead of thinking “this costs £300”, the app shows:

- “This costs 18 work hours”
- “This costs 2.3 work days”
- “This costs 1.4 weeks of work”
- “This equals 3 months of your gym membership”

This helps make spending decisions more intentional.

---

## How to use

### 1. Set your income
Choose one method:

- Net annual salary  
- Net monthly pay  
- Gross salary + take-home %

The calculator converts this into your **hourly take-home pay**.

---

### 2. Set work schedule
Enter:

- Hours per week  
- Days per week  
- Hours per day  

This determines how purchases convert into work time.

---

### 3. Set savings rate
Enter the % of your net pay you can save.

This allows the calculator to estimate:

👉 How long in real calendar time to afford something

---

### 4. Add recurring expenses (optional)
Add monthly costs like:

- Rent
- Subscriptions
- Gym
- Phone
- Bills

These reduce your saving capacity and enable opportunity-cost comparisons.

---

### 5. Add an item
Enter:

- Name
- Price (£)
- Category
- Type (one-off / monthly / yearly)
- Notes (optional)

Click **Add item**.

---

## Understanding results

### Work time
How much labour time the item costs.
